p. Linaro releases monthly binary images of UEFI, based on the Tiaoncore project.

h2. Tianocore baselines

p. The Linaro UEFI tree is based on the following upstream versions of code from Tianocore:

** edk2 at subversion revision 14323
** edk2-fatdriver2 at subversion revision 73
** edk2-buildtools at subversion revision 2579

h2. Platform Support

p. Platforms currently supported include:

** Versatile Express A5/A9/TC1/TC2 and RTSM A9x4 and A15x1, A15 MPCore.
** Samsung Arndale
** TI Beagleboard
